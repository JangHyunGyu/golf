Frequently asked questions

What should I look for in a free golf swing analyzer?

A useful analyzer should accept ordinary phone video, explain the feedback clearly, and cover measurable areas such as posture, tempo, and impact without hiding the result behind a paywall.

Can I use this as an online swing analyser?

Yes. Analyzer and analyser are US and UK spellings for the same tool. Golf Swing Master works online in either case and supports driver and iron swing videos.

Is it really free?

Yes. No signup, no payment, no app install — every analysis feature is unlimited.

What kind of video should I upload?

A 5+ second side-view (P-side) clip of a full driver or iron swing works best. 720p or higher recommended.

How does this compare to a pro lesson?

Quantitative analysis (tempo, posture, plane) is roughly on par with a pro. Course management and mental coaching still need a human coach.

Is my video shared anywhere?

Uploaded clips are discarded right after analysis and not stored on the server.
