ü§ñ What is Golf Swing Analysis?

Our advanced motion analysis engine scans your video frame-by-frame to analyze the mechanics of your swing.

It compares your data with PGA/LPGA Tour Pros to objectively diagnose your Address, Backswing, Impact, Finish, and more.

üèÜ What are Reference Pros?

These are World Class Tour Pros whose data is used as the standard for the analysis engine.

A perfect score of 10 represents their level, and your swing is evaluated relative to this high standard.

Key Reference Pros
  • Men (PGA): Tiger Woods, Rory McIlroy, Scottie Scheffler, Jon Rahm
  • Women (LPGA): Nelly Korda, Jin Young Ko, Lydia Ko, Hyo Joo Kim
üí°
Don't be disappointed! Since you are compared to Tour Pros, your score might be lower than expected. A score of 6-7 is considered excellent, at the level of a single handicapper.

üí° How to Use

  1. Select Club: Choose from Driver, Wood/Hybrid, Iron, Wedge
  2. Select Video: Select or drag & drop the video to analyze
  3. Analysis & Coaching: Click the button to start analysis (takes 1-2 mins)
